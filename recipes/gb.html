Garlic Bread

Warm garlic bread; ready to eat!

Here is a recipe on how to make simple garlic bread. Your friends will think you are a pro! Simply follow these steps until your garlic bread looks like this! Good luck everyone.

Ingredients

  • baguette 1
  • garlic 1 head minced
  • parsley 1/4 cup chopped
  • unsalted butter 1cup
  • parmesan cheese 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Cut the baguette into 1 in pieces
  2. soften the butter and mix it the rest of ingredients
  3. cool the butter and then place 1tbs per 1 in pieces
  4. bake at 375 for 15min
